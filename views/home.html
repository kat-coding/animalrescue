Pet of the Month

Meet "Harry". He's a one-year-old orange tabby cat in excellent health. He was loved and well cared-for but one of the children in his family developed a pet allergy and they couldn't keep him. He is very sweet, and he will cuddle you for hours. But don't let his good temperament fool you--he LOVES to play. With his confident and friendly disposition, Harry has a way of winning over the hearts of everyone he meets. He is truly a unique and unforgettable cat, with a personality that is sure to bring joy and laughter to any household. He was an indoor/outdoor pet, so whatever your preference, he will accommodate.

New Arrival

Welcome George to our rescue site. At first he is a little skeptical of people and his surroundings but once he starts getting into a routine he quickly gets with the program and becomes part of you. He is a gorgeous blonde with beautiful eyes, a tall dog that weighed in at 80 plus pounds plus a tail that curls on his back. He came to us extremely underweight and needed to be fully medically vetted and heartworm negative. We believe he is a Lab/ Great Pyrenees mix and young at close to 2 years old! After he becomes acclimated to a new environment, he is a big ole ham and large clumsy baby! He is very puppy-like but starting to realize how big he is.
